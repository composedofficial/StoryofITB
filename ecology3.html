Green Oasis - Marginal lands provide shelter and food Bramble shrub supports: • Blackbirds, thrushes, chaffinches, starlings, robins, foxes, mice and other small mammals eat the fruits. • Cover for foxes. • Flowers from May to September, fruits from August to October.

GS4 Wet grassland

Sweet floating grass - Sprawling, rooting stems with roughish flat pale-green leaves often floating on surface of water. Bulrushes – Cover for water fowl.
